manufacturing of <a href=\"\/wiki\/Cyclorama_(theater)\" title=\"Cyclorama (theater)\">cycloramas<\/a>, <a href=\"\/wiki\/Theater_drapes_and_stage_curtains\" title=\"Theater drapes and stage id=\"History\">History<\/h2><span class=\"mw-editsection\"><span class=\"mw-editsection-bracket\">[<\/span><a href=\"\/w\/index.php?title=Peroni_(company)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1\" title=\"Edit title=\"Projection screen\">projection screens<\/a> and <a href=\"\/wiki\/Video_projector\" title=\"Video projector\">rear-projection screens<\/a>, these last widely used for <a machineries<\/a>.\r\nThe company is still owned and managed by the founder family<sup id=\"cite&#95;ref-Peroni&#39;s&#95;history&#95;1-1\" class=\"reference\"><a
