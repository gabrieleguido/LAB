**Peroni S.p.A.** is a company headquartered in [Gallarate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gallarate "Gallarate"), [Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italy "Italy"). **Peroni** is an Italian brand that is worldwide known by theatrical productions because of its manufacturing of [cycloramas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyclorama_\(theater\) "Cyclorama \(theater\)"), [theater drapes and stage curtains](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theater_drapes_and_stage_curtains "Theater drapes and stage curtains") and other materials for [scenography](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scenography "Scenography"). 
**Peroni** established a [weaving mill](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Textile_manufacturing "Textile manufacturing") near the end of 18th Century[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peroni_\(company\)#cite_note-Peroni's_history-1) in Gallarate, a city in the main cotton weaving industrial district of [Lombardy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lombardy "Lombardy"), in [Northwest Italy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northwest_Italy "Northwest Italy"). The firm had been producing cotton and linen [blankets](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blanket "Blanket") for more than a hundred years when, at the beginning of 20th Century, it began the manufacturing of big size cotton fabrics for [scenic painting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scenic_painting "Scenic painting") like [canvas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canvas "Canvas") and later cotton [tulle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tulle_netting "Tulle netting") that became its main products. Around 1980 **Peroni** also started the manufacturing of vinyl [projection screens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Projection_screen "Projection screen") and [rear-projection screens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Video_projector "Video projector"), these last widely used for [stage lighting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stage_lighting "Stage lighting") too by theatrical shows, and that of [stage machineries](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stage_machinery "Stage machinery"). The company is still owned and managed by the founder family[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peroni_\(company\)#cite_note-Peroni's_history-1) and nowadays it seems to be constantly involved in the production of important shows as it is witnessed by the many examples that can be watched in its website.
